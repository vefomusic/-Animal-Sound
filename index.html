From COP30 to the stage

I'm VEFO — an artist, activist, and creator who believes art can move people and make great positive change in the world.

My journey has taken me from performing in Canada to across South America to now India. Universities, Festivals, in government work to COP30 in Brazil, and now to Bangalore, where I create music that amplifies animal rights, climate action, and mental health awareness.
